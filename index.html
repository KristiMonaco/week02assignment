A FEW WONDERFUL CITIES

Istanbul, Türkiye

Istanbul is the oldest city in the world that still exists today. The Ayasofya mosque was built nearly 2,700 years ago and still stands. Incredible! The city straddles two continents and is a captivating tapestry of past and present where east meets west. Istanbul's neighborhoods are like a mosaic, each with its own unique character. As you wander the streets, every step you take is a journey through time and culture.

Bogotá, Colombia

Bogotá is nested at 8,660 feet above sea level in the Andes Mountains. It is the largest city in Colombia and a cultural hub known for its historic charm dating back to the Spanish colonial era. It has a bustling urban environment and is known for its thriving street art scene, unique cuisine, diverse population and natural beauty.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is probably the biggest city in the world, and a unique place where tradition and innovation seamlessly coexist, creating a mezmerizing blend of old-world charm and futuristic wonder. I love the crowded nature of the city and all the interesting architecture, fashion, food, shrines, temples and parks.
